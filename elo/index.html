How the Elo update works

Step 1 — Predict. Compute the expected win chance for player A:
Ea = 1 / (1 + 10(Rb − Ra)/400).

Step 2 — Observe. Record the actual score Sa (1 for a win, 0 for a loss).

Step 3 — Update. Move the rating toward the result:
R′a = Ra + K × (Sa − Ea).

A larger K reacts faster to new games; a smaller one keeps ratings stable.
